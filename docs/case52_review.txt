Case 52 -- Adult Medical Critical (Renal/Diabetes Kussmaul Acidosis)

Step 1
A 38-year-old woman is brought to the emergency department because of lethargy and deep rapid breathing.

While breathing room air, the following are noted:
HR 116/min
RR 32/min
BP 144/86 mm Hg
SpO2 89%
EtCO2 30 mm Hg

Which of the following should be evaluated initially? SELECT AS MANY AS INDICATED (MAX 4).

Options
A. Assess the breathing pattern
B. Obtain an ABG
C. Review the chemistry panel and glucose
D. Review urine output and ketones
E. Review the CBC
F. Delay treatment until the full diagnostic panel is completed
G. Transfer to a low-acuity bed before stabilization

Step 2
